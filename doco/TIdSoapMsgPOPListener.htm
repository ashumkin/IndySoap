TIdSoapMsgPOPListener

 

TIdSoapMsgPOPListener is a POP client that processes incoming POP3 EMail messages as SOAP messages. You must call the procedure PollForSoapMessages to check for incoming email messages. The SOAP messages are handled in the thread that calls PollForSoapMessages.
